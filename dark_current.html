Dark Current in Silicon Detectors

Project Overview

Characterizing and reducing dark current in silicon detectors for improved sensitivity in astronomical instruments.

Objectives

  • Measure and analyze dark current in various detector setups.
  • Develop techniques to minimize dark current for space applications.
