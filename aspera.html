Aspera – NASA Pioneers SmallSat

Project Overview

Aspera SmallSat will study the diffuse O VI emission at 103.2 nm from the warm-hot gas in the circumgalactic medium (CGM) of nearby galaxies.

Objectives

  • Investigate the properties of the CGM using ultraviolet spectroscopy.
  • Develop and deploy a small satellite for astrophysical research.
